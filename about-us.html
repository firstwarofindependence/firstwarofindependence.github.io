What we are

Cluster Innovation Center is the flagship college of the University of Delhi. It was established in the year 2011. There are 2 UG courses that are taught here, namely- BA Hons in Humanities and Social Sciences and B.Tech in Innovation with Mathematics and IT. There is also a PG course namely- Masters in Mathematical Education. Former coordinator of BA Hons in Humanities and Social Sciences course and recipient of Aruna Asaf Ali Chair, Dr. Sukrita Paul Kumar is the founder and mentor of this project. This project has been introduced in the curriculum since its inception in 2014. We have covered aspects like architecture, folklore, monuments, cultural values etc. The project was not limited to one city. The research went through different cities where the revolt broke out and the different aspects of those cities were brought into limelight by the following members hailing from different streams into one arena:
